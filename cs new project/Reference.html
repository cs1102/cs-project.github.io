Discovery:

We introduce four main Chinese input methods including stroke count, pinyin, voice, and Cangjie. Pinyin and voice input are based on pronunciation, while the stroke count and Cangjie approaches are based on character structure. Accuracy and attention to detail are necessary for all modalities. Special keyboard layouts and further training and knowledge are necessary for voice input and Cangjie input. Despite their differences, both approaches are crucial for communicating in and learning Chinese in the modern and globalized world.
